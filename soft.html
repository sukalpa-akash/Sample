CNC PROGRAMMING

Create offline CNC programs for stage and final machining processes. Offline programming contains CNC programs, selection of cutting tools, Tool cards, setup instructions. All the programs will be simulated and validated by verification software to ensure collision-free deliverables.

Capable of delivering CNC programs for Turn, Turn-mill up to 5 Axis machine configurations and compatible with all types of machine controls.

STACS is responsible to prove programs on the machine and handover to production teams by gaining approval from customers.

CMM PROGRAMMING

Create offline CMM programming for in-process and Final stage inspection. The activities including developing a measurement strategy, decide the number of set-ups, calibration & selection of probes, optimized probe points, speed & travel.

Ensure CMM programs are meeting engineering requirements, online prove-out and conduct Gauge Repeatability & Reproducibility (Gauge R&R).

QUALITY & PPAP
